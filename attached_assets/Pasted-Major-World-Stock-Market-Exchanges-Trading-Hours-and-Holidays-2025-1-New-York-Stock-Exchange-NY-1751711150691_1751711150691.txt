Major World Stock Market Exchanges: Trading Hours and Holidays (2025)
1. New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) - United States

    Local Time: 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 7:00 PM – 1:30 AM (next day); shifts to 6:30 PM – 1:00 AM during US DST (Mar 9–Nov 2)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Martin Luther King Jr. Day (Jan 20), Presidents’ Day (Feb 17), Good Friday (Apr 18), Memorial Day (May 26), Juneteenth (Jun 19), Independence Day (Jul 4), Labor Day (Sep 1), Thanksgiving (Nov 27), Christmas (Dec 25). Early closures (1:00 PM ET) on Jul 3, Nov 28 (Black Friday), Dec 24 (Christmas Eve).
    Notes: No lunch break. Pre-market (4:00 AM – 9:30 AM ET) and after-hours (4:00 PM – 8:00 PM ET) trading available.

2. Nasdaq - United States

    Local Time: 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 7:00 PM – 1:30 AM (next day); shifts to 6:30 PM – 1:00 AM during US DST
    Holidays: Same as NYSE.
    Notes: Same hours and extended trading as NYSE.

3. London Stock Exchange (LSE) - United Kingdom

    Local Time: 8:00 AM – 4:30 PM GMT (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 1:30 PM – 10:00 PM; shifts to 12:30 PM – 9:00 PM during UK DST (Mar 30–Oct 26, BST)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Good Friday (Apr 18), Easter Monday (Apr 21), Early May Bank Holiday (May 5), Spring Bank Holiday (May 26), Summer Bank Holiday (Aug 25), Christmas (Dec 25), Boxing Day (Dec 26). Early closures (12:30 PM GMT) on Dec 24, Dec 31.
    Notes: No lunch break. Two-minute break at noon.

4. Euronext (Paris, Amsterdam, Brussels, Lisbon, Dublin) - Europe

    Local Time: 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM CET (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 1:30 PM – 10:00 PM; shifts to 12:30 PM – 9:00 PM during EU DST (Mar 30–Oct 26, CEST)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Good Friday (Apr 18), Easter Monday (Apr 21), Labour Day (May 1), Christmas (Dec 25), Boxing Day (Dec 26). Half-day trading on Dec 24, Dec 31 (e.g., Dublin equities close 12:28–12:30 PM, ETFs at 1:00 PM).
    Notes: No lunch break. Closing times vary slightly by market.

5. Shanghai Stock Exchange (SSE) - China

    Local Time: 9:30 AM – 3:00 PM CST (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 7:00 AM – 12:30 PM (CST is UTC+8, 2.5-hour difference)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1–3), Chinese New Year (Jan 28–Feb 3), Qingming Festival (Apr 4–6), Labour Day (May 1–5), Dragon Boat Festival (May 31–Jun 2), Mid-Autumn Festival (Oct 6–8), National Day (Oct 1–7).
    Notes: Lunch break from 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM CST.

6. Shenzhen Stock Exchange (SZSE) - China

    Local Time: 9:30 AM – 3:00 PM CST (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 7:00 AM – 12:30 PM
    Holidays: Same as SSE.
    Notes: Lunch break from 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM CST.

7. Hong Kong Stock Exchange (HKEX) - Hong Kong

    Local Time: 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM HKT (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 7:00 AM – 1:30 PM (HKT is UTC+8)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Lunar New Year (Jan 28–30), Qingming Festival (Apr 4), Good Friday (Apr 18), Easter Monday (Apr 21), Labour Day (May 1), Buddha’s Birthday (May 12), Dragon Boat Festival (May 31), HKSAR Establishment Day (Jul 1), Mid-Autumn Festival (Oct 7), National Day (Oct 1), Chung Yeung Festival (Oct 29), Christmas (Dec 25), Boxing Day (Dec 26).
    Notes: Lunch break from 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM HKT.

8. Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) - Japan

    Local Time: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM JST (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 5:30 AM – 11:30 AM (JST is UTC+9, 3.5-hour difference)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1–3), Coming of Age Day (Jan 13), National Foundation Day (Feb 11), Emperor’s Birthday (Feb 23), Vernal Equinox (Mar 20), Showa Day (Apr 29), Constitution Memorial Day (May 3), Greenery Day (May 4), Children’s Day (May 5), Marine Day (Jul 21), Mountain Day (Aug 11), Respect for the Aged Day (Sep 15), Autumnal Equinox (Sep 23), Sports Day (Oct 13), Culture Day (Nov 3), Labor Thanksgiving Day (Nov 24).
    Notes: Lunch break from 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM JST.

9. Saudi Stock Exchange (Tadawul) - Saudi Arabia

    Local Time: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM AST (Sunday–Thursday)
    IST: 12:30 PM – 5:30 PM (AST is UTC+3, 2.5-hour difference)
    Holidays: Eid al-Fitr (Mar 30–Apr 2, estimated), Eid al-Adha (Jun 6–9, estimated), Saudi National Day (Sep 23).
    Notes: Operates Sunday–Thursday (Friday–Saturday weekend).

10. Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX) - Canada

    Local Time: 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 7:00 PM – 1:30 AM (next day); shifts to 6:30 PM – 1:00 AM during US DST
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Family Day (Feb 17), Good Friday (Apr 18), Victoria Day (May 19), Canada Day (Jul 1), Civic Holiday (Aug 4), Labour Day (Sep 1), National Day for Truth and Reconciliation (Sep 30, observed), Thanksgiving (Oct 13), Christmas (Dec 25), Boxing Day (Dec 26).
    Notes: No lunch break. Aligns with NYSE/Nasdaq.

11. National Stock Exchange (NSE) - India

    Segments: Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives, Commodity Derivatives
    Local Time:
        Pre-Open: 9:00 AM – 9:15 AM IST
        Regular Trading: 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM IST (Monday–Friday)
        Post-Close: 3:40 PM – 4:00 PM IST
    IST: Same as local
    Holidays: Republic Day (Jan 26), Mahashivratri (Feb 26), Holi (Mar 14), Ram Navami (Apr 8), Mahavir Jayanti (Apr 10), Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Jayanti (Apr 14), Good Friday (Apr 18), Id-Ul-Fitr (Mar 31), Maharashtra Day (May 1), Bakri Id (Jun 7), Independence Day (Aug 15), Ganesh Chaturthi (Aug 27), Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti (Oct 2), Diwali-Laxmi Pujan (Oct 21, Muhurat Trading only), Guru Nanak Jayanti (Nov 5), Christmas (Dec 25). Muhurat Trading: Oct 21, ~6:15 PM – 7:15 PM IST (exact times TBD).
    Notes: No lunch break. Five holidays fall on weekends (e.g., Republic Day).

12. Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) - India

    Segments: Equity, Equity Derivatives, Currency Derivatives
    Local Time: Same as NSE (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM IST, pre-open 9:00 AM – 9:15 AM)
    IST: Same as local
    Holidays: Same as NSE. Muhurat Trading on Oct 21.
    Notes: Identical to NSE schedule.

13. Multi Commodity Exchange (MCX) - India

    Segments: Commodity Futures (metals, energy, agriculture)
    Local Time:
        Morning: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM IST
        Evening: 5:00 PM – 11:30 PM IST (11:55 PM during US DST, Mar 9–Nov 2)
        Agricultural Commodities: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM IST
    IST: Same as local
    Holidays: Same as NSE. Evening session may have adjusted closures.
    Notes: Extended hours for non-agricultural commodities.

14. NSE Commodity Derivatives - India

    Segments: Commodity Futures
    Local Time: Aligns with MCX (9:00 AM – 5:00 PM IST for agricultural; 9:00 AM – 11:30 PM/11:55 PM IST for non-agricultural)
    IST: Same as local
    Holidays: Same as NSE.
    Notes: Part of NSE’s derivatives platform.

15. NSE Currency Derivatives - India

    Segments: Currency Futures, Options (USD-INR, etc.)
    Local Time: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM IST (Monday–Friday)
    IST: Same as local
    Holidays: Same as NSE.
    Notes: No evening session.

16. Singapore Exchange (SGX) - Singapore

    Local Time:
        Pre-Open: 8:30 AM – 8:59 AM SGT
        Regular Trading: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM SGT (Monday–Friday)
        Closing Auction: 5:00 PM – 5:06 PM SGT
    IST: 6:00 AM – 2:36 PM (SGT is UTC+8, 2.5-hour difference)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Chinese New Year (Jan 29–30), Good Friday (Apr 18), Labour Day (May 1), Hari Raya Puasa (Mar 31), Vesak Day (May 12), Hari Raya Haji (Jun 7), National Day (Aug 9), Deepavali (Oct 20), Christmas (Dec 25). Half-day trading (12:00 PM SGT) on Jan 28, Dec 24, Dec 31.
    Notes: No lunch break.

17. Deutsche Börse (Xetra) - Germany

    Local Time: 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM CET (Monday–Friday)
    IST: 1:30 PM – 10:00 PM; shifts to 12:30 PM – 9:00 PM during EU DST (Mar 30–Oct 26, CEST)
    Holidays: New Year’s Day (Jan 1), Good Friday (Apr 18), Easter Monday (Apr 21), Labour Day (May 1), Christmas Eve (Dec 24), Christmas (Dec 25), Boxing Day (Dec 26), New Year’s Eve (Dec 31).
    Notes: No lunch break. Xetra is the primary platform for German equities.

18. Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) - Bangladesh

    Local Time: 10:00 AM – 2:30 PM BDT (Sunday–Thursday)
    IST: 9:30 AM – 2:00 PM (BDT is UTC+6, 30-minute difference)
    Holidays: Independence Day (Mar 26), Bengali New Year (Apr 14), May Day (May 1), Eid-ul-Fitr (Mar 31, estimated), Eid-ul-Adha (Jun 7, estimated), National Mourning Day (Aug 15), Durga Puja (Oct 10), Victory Day (Dec 16), Christmas (Dec 25). Lunar holiday dates depend on sightings.
    Notes: Sunday–Thursday trading (Friday–Saturday weekend).
